Hello! My name is Rishi (he/him). I'm a senior at the University of Pennsylvania studying mathematics under the supervision of Prof. Dennis DeTurck.

I am currently applying to PhD programs, with a primary focus on differential geometry, though I am also interested in probability theory.

Aside from math, I also enjoy lifting and playing the flute; I am currently working on Prokofiev's Sonata in D Major.
